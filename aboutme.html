Introduction

I feel like I'm not quite grown up yet, even though I've been around for about three decades. My wife would probably agree with that, too. I searched for a long time for something to really put my mind too and couldn't find anything that caught my attention the right way. Recently I've been working for a radio station, which is a lot of fun but hardly rewarding. Now I've started coding and it seems like a good fit. Even the simplest things feel like problems that need to be solved, and if there's anything I love it's solving problems.

Where I'm From

I grew up as a Navy brat and moved all over the East Coast, as far north as Newport, RI and as far south as Key West, FL. My family is from Winston-Salem, NC, and we moved back here when I was 14. I've been here ever since, with the exception of a few minor interruptions. It's not a bad place to live, not at all.
